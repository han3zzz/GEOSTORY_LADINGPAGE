// Process

How GeoStory Works

Three steps to immortalize your story on the blockchain — tied to a place, visible to the world.

// Step 01
Connect Your Wallet

Authenticate with Petra (Aptos), MetaMask (EVM), or try Demo Mode. Your wallet is your identity — no email, no password.

// Step 02
Pin Your Story

Drop a pin anywhere on the globe. Add a title, photo, mood, and category. Each story is geotagged with precise coordinates.

// Step 03
Publish to Shelby

Your story is stored permanently on Shelby Protocol — immutable, censorship-resistant, and retrievable forever on the decentralized web.
